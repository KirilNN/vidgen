## 1. UNDERLORD — AI Video Agent / "Vibe Editing"

**One-line positioning:** Agentic AI video co-editor that reads your content, plans tasks, and executes edits on command.

### Capabilities
- **Natural-language video editing:** Type commands like "Edit this down to 2 minutes, focusing on when I talked about Freud" or "Make the trailer for this webinar recording"
- **Script writing & feedback:** Writes scripts from prompts, edits based on feedback, or reviews your existing script and gives feedback
- **Content conversion:** Turns docs, slides, blog posts, LinkedIn posts, screen recordings, and code of conduct documents into video content
- **Bulk edits:** Executes multiple tedious edits simultaneously (e.g., center every speaker, bleep language, add lower thirds across a whole video)
- **Context-aware suggestions:** Reads your script and video content to decide what to do next; makes suggestions proactively
- **Smart cutting:** Can cut to "most exciting parts" or "best moments for maximum engagement"
- **Stock media placement:** Fills in media placeholders with stock footage automatically
- **Camera layout automation:** Adds camera layouts every time a specific feature/topic is referenced
- **Speaker isolation:** Copies all parts where a specific speaker is talking into a new composition
- **Animated callouts:** Adds animated callouts to specific content segments (e.g., ethics training principles)
- **Zoom / jump-cut masking:** Adds zooms to hide jump cuts
- **Chat history:** Persists per-user, per-project; syncs across browsers and devices; auto-titles past sessions
- **Context picker:** `@` button lets you pin files, scenes, timestamps, or layers to a request so Underlord targets the right elements
- **Improved edit review:** After every editing turn, a second AI pass checks the diff against what was requested, looking for unintended deletions, missed targets, overcorrections, or out-of-scope operations — auto-corrects if something's wrong
- **Reasoning models:** Uses reasoning models for complex tasks (script writing, episode planning, multi-step edits)
- **API access:** Available via Descript API (open beta); supports Model Context Protocol (MCP) connections for use with Claude, ChatGPT, etc.

### Use Case Examples (Verbatim from page)
- "This how-to video has good content but bad energy—HELP!"
- "Turn my CEO's rant about AI & productivity into a thought leadership video"
- "Create a '5 common mistakes dentists make' video using the examples in this LinkedIn post"
- "Create a :30 video for Facebook about…"
- "Create a short video reviewing our code of conduct (below), but make it like a TED Talk from 2009"
- "Whip up an avatar-hosted explainer. Edit down a webinar and create promo clips."

### Sub-Features (also available manually)
- Text-based editing (video editing via transcript)
- Scenes + layouts (design-like canvas)
- AI actions (Eye Contact, Studio Sound, Remove Filler Words, Remove Retakes)

### Tier Availability
- Free: Limited use
- Hobbyist: Access (basic)
- Creator: Full access (20+ AI tools included)
- Business/Enterprise: Full access

**One-line positioning:** AI repairs mismatched audio tones, enhances flat/boring dialogue, and eliminates background noise — making impossible edits sound seamless.

### Capabilities
- **Smooth over awkward edits:** When editing audio creates a tone mismatch (cut-and-paste causes jarring tonal jump), Regenerate recreates the speaker's voice with AI and matches surrounding tone so the edit sounds natural
- **Liven up lackluster dialogue:** Injects energy and life into flat/boring performances — turns "deathly boring" segments into "truly engaging" ones without re-recording
- **Eliminate annoying noise:** Removes background noise (leaf blowers, dogs, etc.) at the segment level — one-click fix
- **Works on podcasts, video, and screen recordings**
- **Voice regeneration:** Recreates speaker's voice with AI to fill gaps created by edits

### Important Distinction from Studio Sound & Video Regenerate
- **Studio Sound** = broad audio cleanup on an entire clip (noise reduction + enhancement)
- **Regenerate Speech** = targeted repair at edit points + tone matching + dialogue energy injection; also operates on entire clips for noise
- **Video Regenerate** = also changes mouth/lip sync to match new words (audio + video)
- Regenerate Speech = **audio only** (no lip sync, no new words)

### Use Cases
- Podcasts: stitch edits seamlessly, save hours
- Video: fix awkward edits, remove noise on voiceovers or speech audio
- Screen recordings: narrate imperfectly and fix everything in post

### How It Works
- Identifies tone/quality mismatch around edit points
- Regenerates voice audio to bridge the gap
- Result: sounds like it was recorded in one continuous take
